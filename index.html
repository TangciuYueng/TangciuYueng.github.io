CMPhysBench: A Benchmark for Evaluating Large Language Models in Condensed Matter Physics
Weida Wang *, Dongchen Huang *, Jiatong Li *, Tengchao Yang *, Ziyang Zheng, Di Zhang, et al.
under review (submitted to ICLR), Aug. 2025.

We propose CMPhysBench, a benchmark to evaluate LLM proficiency in condensed matter physics using over 520 calculation problems. This work also introduces the SEED score, a new metric for robustly evaluating mathematical expression similarity.
MMTutorBench: The First Multimodal Benchmark for AI Math Tutoring
Tengchao Yang *, Sichen Guo *, Mengzhao Jia, Jiaming Su, Yuanyang Liu, Zhihan Zhang, and Meng Jiang.
under review (submitted to ACL), Oct. 2025.

We introduce MMTutorBench, the first multimodal benchmark for AI math tutoring , which evaluates an AI's ability to diagnose handwritten work and provide structured, step-by-step guidance.
A-MemGuard: A Proactive Defense Framework for LLM-Based Agent Memory
Qianshan Wei *, Tengchao Yang *, Yaochen Wang *, Xinfeng Li, Lijun Li, Zhenfei Yin, et al.
under review (submitted to ICLR), Oct. 2025.

We propose A-MemGuard, a proactive defense for LLM agent memory that detects context-aware attacks via consensus validation. It uses a dual-memory structure to learn from failures, breaking self-reinforcing error cycles.
